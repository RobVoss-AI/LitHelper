The Problem with Literature Discovery

🔍

Scattered Across Platforms

Google Scholar for search, Research Rabbit for graphs, Litmaps for timelines, Zotero for management. Four tools, four workflows, zero integration.

💰

Paywalls & Limits

Research Rabbit merged into Litmaps. Free tiers are limited. Premium plans add up. Your discovery workflow shouldn't depend on a subscription.

🚫

No Breadcrumbs

You find a great paper, but can't retrace how you got there. Discovery paths are lost the moment you close a tab.

Features

Everything You Need in One App

Free, open-source, and built by a researcher who uses these tools every day.

🔎

Smart Paper Search

Search 250M+ works via OpenAlex. See citations, year, open access status, and authors at a glance. Cmd+K to jump to search anytime.

🛰

Citation Network Graphs

Interactive force-directed graphs. Nodes sized by citation count, colored by year. Right-click to expand. Toggle graph, scatter, or split view.

🌱

Multi-Seed Discovery

Add 3-5 seed papers, then find overlooked work via co-citation analysis or bibliographic coupling. Ranked by overlap score.

📂

Collections & Export

Organize papers into named collections. Export as BibTeX, RIS, or shareable JSON bundles. One-click save from any paper card.

👥

Author Tracking

Follow researchers you care about. Get notified when they publish new work. View publication timelines and co-author networks.

🔔

Monitored Searches

Save any search query and let LitHelper check for new matching papers on your schedule. Never miss relevant new work.

🧭

Research Trails

Record your exploration path. Navigate back and forward through breadcrumbs. Save trails to revisit your exact research journey.

📚

Zotero Integration

Two-way sync with your Zotero library. Pull collections in, push discoveries back. Import .bib and .ris files with OpenAlex resolution.

How It Works

Up and Running in Minutes

No API keys, no accounts, no configuration. Just download and start discovering.

Download & Launch

Download LitHelper for your platform. Double-click to open. The backend starts automatically — no terminal needed.

Search & Explore

Search for any topic or paper. Click "Explore" to build a citation graph. Right-click nodes to expand the network in any direction.

Discover & Save

Use multi-seed discovery to find overlooked papers. Save everything into collections. Export to Zotero, BibTeX, or share with colleagues.

Stay Current

Track authors, monitor searches, and record your trails. LitHelper keeps working for you even when you're not actively searching.
